Facial Recognition App

The app I made as part of my final year project at university. The purpose of the app is to guess a user's emotion from thier facial expression and then direct them to a music playlist based on that emotion; Happy, for example! This was a great oppertunity to learn a language I has no experience in and experiment with APIs.

Developed 2022

The app was made for andriod phones using kotlin, developed in andriod studio. The app uses Google's ML Kit to find facial landmarks which are used in the emotion classification process.

Some images here were captured within the andriod studio emulator.

Espace Room Game

This game was my first experience of developing in Unity. The aim was to develop the game with the theme and feel of an escape room.

Developed 2022

The game is a race against time to find whatever item is the 'odd one out' in each room with hints to help the player.

I tried to stick to a consistent (quite blocky) look for all of the objects inside the house. It features user interaction with objects, collectible items and collision detecion. I also used this game to learn how to create my own models, work with menus, particle effects and multiple scenes.
